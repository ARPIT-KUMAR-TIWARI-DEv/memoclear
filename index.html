Alpha-GPC

The most bioavailable form of choline available, Alpha-GPC is a precursor to acetylcholine — your brain's primary learning and memory neurotransmitter. It crosses the blood-brain barrier efficiently, delivering immediate boosts to mental clarity, focus, and information processing speed.

Ginkgo Biloba

One of the most researched botanicals for cognitive health, Ginkgo dramatically improves cerebral blood flow and possesses powerful antioxidant properties. Acetyl-L-Carnitine

ALCAR is a mitochondrial energizer that directly fuels brain cells with clean, sustained energy. It also acts as an antioxidant against neuronal damage while boosting acetylcholine activity. Ashwagandha KSM-66®

L-Theanine

Found naturally in green tea, L-Theanine promotes calm, focused alertness by increasing alpha brain waves without sedation. When combined with the formula's other ingredients, it sharpens concentration, reduces mind-wandering, and creates the ideal cognitive state for deep work and learning.

Rhodiola Rosea

An elite adaptogenic herb used by Soviet athletes and cosmonauts, Rhodiola dramatically reduces mental and physical fatigue under high-stress conditions. Vitamin B Complex (B6, B9, B12)

Omega-3 DHA

DHA comprises 97% of the omega-3 fatty acids in your brain and is the primary structural fat in neuronal membranes. Huperzine A

Derived from Chinese club moss, Huperzine A is an acetylcholinesterase inhibitor — meaning it prevents the enzyme that breaks down acetylcholine from doing so too quickly.
